# About Quantum Automated Program Repair

Quantum Automated Program Repair (Quantum APR) is an emerging research area focused on automatically detecting, diagnosing, and repairing bugs in quantum software.

As quantum computing systems continue to grow in complexity, developing reliable quantum programs becomes increasingly challenging.

Research in Quantum APR explores topics such as:

- Quantum bug detection
- Automated repair generation
- Program validation and verification
- Quantum software testing
- Benchmark creation and evaluation
- Machine learning for quantum software engineering

This website serves as a community hub for Quantum APR research projects, resources, and open-source implementations.
